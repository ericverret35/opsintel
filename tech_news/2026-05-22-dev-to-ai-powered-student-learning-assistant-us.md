## AI Powered Student Learning Assistant Using Gemma 4

**Source**: Dev.to

  This is a submission for the  Gemma 4 Challenge   

 
  
  
  What I Built
 

 I built a Smart Study Assistant using Gemma 4 that helps students learn more efficiently. The assistant can summarize notes, answer questions, create quizzes, and explain difficult topics in simple language. 

 The goal is to reduce study stress and provide quick learning support for students.
