## Why I Built Opposition Into My AI Council

**Source**: Dev.to

 One thing I became increasingly uncomfortable with was letting the AI architect decide how its own proposal should be challenged. 

 It was not because I thought the Architect was weak. Most of the time, it was capable, careful, and grounded in the source. But once it had formed the first coherent interpretation of a task, later reviews could still remain inside that interpretation. 

 Several agents could participate and the process could look independent, while the original framing continued
