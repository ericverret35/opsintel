## The CPU Is Back in the Stack — and Nobody Budgeted for It

**Source**: Dev.to

 The CPU never left the stack. It was reclassified — quietly, and incorrectly — as support compute. Something that fed the GPU, scheduled around the GPU, and otherwise stayed out of the way while the GPU did the "real" work. That classification held for exactly as long as AI workloads were big, monolithic training and inference jobs with predictable shapes. It does not hold for agentic systems.
