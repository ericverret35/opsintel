We’re aware of an issue affecting Workflows that include 'refer to a message link'. Users with these workflows are unable to send a message to a channel, and will receive an ‘Input Validation Error’ when trying to perform this step. We’re actively investigating this issue and will provide an update in 30 minutes or sooner if more information becomes available.&nbsp;

More details: https://slack-status.com/2026-07/e82ee127d8bed735
